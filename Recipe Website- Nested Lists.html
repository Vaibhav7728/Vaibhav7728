Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  2. 1 cup brown sugar
  3. 1/2 cup white sugar
  4. 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. 3 cups all-purpose flour
  6. 1 tsp baking soda
  7. 1/2 tsp salt
  8. 2 cups chocolate chips

Cooking Steps

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • In a large bowl, mix together the melted butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth.
  • Add the eggs and vanilla extract, and mix until well combined.
  • In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  • Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  • Fold in the chocolate chips.
  • Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
