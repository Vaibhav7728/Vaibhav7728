Technology Terms

Algorithm
Part of Speech: Noun
Definition: A sequence of instructions designed to perform a specific task or solve a problem.
Artificial Intelligence (AI)
Part of Speech: Noun
Definition: The simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems.
Blockchain
Part of Speech: Noun
Definition: A decentralized digital ledger that records transactions across many computers in a way that the registered transactions cannot be altered retroactively.
Cloud Computing
Part of Speech: Noun
Definition: The delivery of computing services over the internet, including storage, processing, and software.
